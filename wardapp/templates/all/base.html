HIRDO is non-governmental and non-profit organization. It was established by a group of talented Somali professional and intellectuals on 16th July 2007 to address and overcome the problems of poverty, famine, disease, livelihood improvement, injustice, illiteracy, gender balance, HIV/AIDS awareness rising and war. HIRDO is registered in both Jubbaland and Federal ministries of interior & national security as a non-governmental and non-profit Organization.
